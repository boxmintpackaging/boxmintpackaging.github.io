Got Questions? We Have Answers

Everything you need to know before starting your custom packaging journey

What types of packaging do you specialize in?

We craft everything from luxury rigid boxes and elegant mailers to eco-friendly kraft packaging. Our 30+ categories cover beauty, food, retail, gifts, electronics, and specialty items.

How does the Custom Design Service work?

Simply fill out our design request form with your brand details, style preferences, and requirements. Our expert designers will create 2-3 custom mockups within 24-48 hours completely FREE with your order!

Do you ship internationally?

Absolutely! We deliver worldwide including USA, Canada, UK, Europe, Australia, and beyond. Shipping is complimentary.

Is there a minimum order requirement?

Zero minimums! Whether you need 10 boxes or 10,000, we treat every order with the same premium care.

How long does production take?

Standard production runs 8-10 business days from design approval. Rush orders available.
